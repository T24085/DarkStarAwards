About the Competition

The Dark Star Awards spotlight visionary filmmakers, designers, and storytellers who push creative boundaries. Submit your most daring work and join a showcase judged by industry veterans and community leaders.

Who Can Enter

Open to solo creators and teams across film, digital art, animation, and interactive media.

What We Value

Bold concepts, polished execution, and stories that resonate with the Dark Star ethos.

Why Participate

Gain recognition, connect with peers, and inspire the next wave of creative pioneers.

How to Submit

  1. Prepare your entry: export your film or interactive demo as a shareable link (e.g., Vimeo, YouTube, or hosted build).
  2. Gather your assets: include a 200-word project description and 2–3 high-resolution stills.
  3. Upload: place your files in a single folder and compress it into a .zip archive.
  4. Send: upload the archive using the competition portal or share a secure download link.

Need help? Reach out to awards@darkstar.com for guidance.
